2. **Include Numbers:** "saves 2 hours/week" (not "saves time")
3. **Provide Context:** "This happened with [client type] in [phase]"
4. **Make It Reusable:** "This pattern applies to all dropshipping stores" (or all Shopify stores)
5. **Connect to Impact:** "This improved conversions by 15%" (why does it matter?)
6. **Document the Why:** "This works because [reason]" (not just the what) ---
